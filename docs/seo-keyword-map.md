# SEO Keyword Map

Organic search foundation for HisWay Press or Print.  
**These are seed topics only.** They require validation before any content expansion. Do not treat them as guaranteed ranking opportunities, and do not promise first-place Google results.

## Validation sources required

Before prioritizing or creating new pages, validate each seed group with:

1. Google Search Console (once the live site is connected)
2. Google Keyword Planner
3. Current Google search results for San Antonio queries
4. Competitor research (local sign/print/apparel shops)
5. Actual customer language from quotes, calls, and door-to-door conversations
6. Google Business Profile search-term insights

Until validated, use these seeds only to guide clear page titles, meta descriptions, and internal linking—not to generate thin or duplicate pages.

## Metadata improvement rules

- Keep titles human and specific; include San Antonio only where natural.
- Do not keyword-stuff H1s or body copy.
- Improve meta descriptions only when they clarify service + locality + next step.
- Do not create thin city/service combo pages in this phase.
- Flip `company.isConceptSite` to `false` only after HisWay adopts the site; indexing while still a concept would compete with the live site.
