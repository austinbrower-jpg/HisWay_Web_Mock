# Content and Photo Intake

Structured workflow for collecting HisWay-owned media after owner approval.  
**Do not scrape Google Images, Instagram, Facebook, or any third-party site.**  
Only use business-owned originals or files with confirmed permission.

## Why original files matter

Prefer camera originals, phone originals in highest quality, or design exports over compressed social downloads. Social platforms strip EXIF data, reduce resolution, and introduce compression artifacts that look soft on large hero and gallery placements.

## Capture guidelines

1. Shoot before and after when possible (blank surface → finished install).
2. Include one wide context shot and one detail shot for every finished job.
3. Keep lighting honest; avoid heavy filters that cannot be reused on the website.
4. Record customer permission in writing (text, email, or signed release) before naming the customer or showing identifiable storefronts when required.
5. Store originals in a dated project folder; publish web-sized derivatives only after approval.

## Website handoff

1. Confirm ownership and permission.
2. Choose the placement using `docs/placeholder-replacement-map.md`.
3. Export web derivatives (see recommended dimensions in the replacement map).
4. Update the matching path under `public/images/` and any data-file references.
5. Add or refresh alt text in the component or data file.
6. Optionally reuse the same caption for Google Business Profile and social posts.

## Out of scope for this phase

- Scraping or downloading third-party images
- Replacing placeholders without verified ownership
- Fabricating project captions or customer stories
