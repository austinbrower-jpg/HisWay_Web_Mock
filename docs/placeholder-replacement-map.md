## Replacement rules

1. Confirm ownership and customer permission before publishing identifiable work.
2. Prefer replacing the file at the same path; update extensions in data/components if switching from SVG to JPG/WebP.
3. Do not invent captions, customer names, or results.
4. After replacement, reuse approved assets for Google Business Profile and social posts using the intake checklist.
